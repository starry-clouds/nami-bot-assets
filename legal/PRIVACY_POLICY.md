# Nami Bot Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-27

This policy explains the basic data practices for Nami Bot.

## What we collect
We collect limited account and gameplay data needed to run the bot, including your Discord user ID and game progress.

## How we use it
We use data to run bot features, save progress, and keep the service working.

## Sharing
We do not sell your data.

We may share limited data with service providers that host the bot, or when required by law.

## Retention
We keep data only as long as needed to operate and support the bot.

## Security
We use reasonable safeguards, but no system is completely secure.

## Changes
We may update this policy. Updates are effective when posted.
